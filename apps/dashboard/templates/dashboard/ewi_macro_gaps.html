Macroprudential EWIs from Credit, Property, and Market Gaps

Borio (2009) constructed a macroeconomic early warning indicator framework to predict banking sector crises using the property price gap, the (real) equity price gap, and the credit gap. The authors argue that the existence of unusally rapid credit growth and asset prices indicate the build-up of financial imbalances that raise the likelihood of subsequent financial distress.
